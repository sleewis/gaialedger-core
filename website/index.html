About GaiaLedger

A currency rooted in community & the planet

GaiaLedger is an open-source cryptocurrency initiative led by the GaiaLedger Community Foundation. Inspired by Gaia — the ancient concept of Earth as a living, interconnected system — we believe money should serve people and the planet, not the other way around.

Built on a proven, battle-tested codebase, GaiaLedger combines the security of decentralized ledger technology with a community that cares deeply about where this technology takes us.

🌍

Powered by a global network of nodes, maintained by people who believe in a fairer financial future.

Why GaiaLedger

Built different, by design

Get started

How to join the network

Getting started with GaiaLedger is straightforward — no technical background required.

Download the wallet

Get the official GaiaLedger wallet for your operating system from our GitHub releases page.

Set up your address

Create your personal wallet address. Your keys stay on your device — you are always in control.

Connect to the network

The wallet automatically connects to the peer-to-peer network and begins syncing the blockchain.

Join the community

Introduce yourself, ask questions, and help shape the future of GaiaLedger alongside the foundation.
